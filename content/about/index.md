I am a software engineer living in Santa Barbara. 

In 2018, I received a Bachelor of Arts in English and computer science with a minor in Chinese from the University of California, Berkeley. My undergraduate honors thesis on translating the poetry of Du Mu was awarded the Dennis Lamb Prize. 

While at Berkeley, I designed and typeset issues 45 to 48 of [*Berkeley Poetry Review*](https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~bpr/) and developed a template system that was used for subsequent issues. I also designed the experimental publication [*Room 1005.999*](http://www.roomonethousand.com/7-issue-1005999) of Berkeley’s architecture journal, Room One Thousand, and worked as a tutor for the computer science department and as a designer and developer for the [Brueghel Family Websites](http://www.brueghelfamily.net/). 

This website serves as an archive of my writing, translations, and photography.
