---
title: Resources for Sinology
--- 
This is a list of resources for sinology, mostly lexicographic reference works. 

Character Studies 
* [漢語多功能字庫 Multi-function Chinese Character Database](https://humanum.arts.cuhk.edu.hk/Lexis/lexi-mf/). Includes character form explanations, images of ancient character forms, topolectal pronunciations, place names containing the character, etc. Link available to the corresponding entry in a scan of the Kangxi dictionary. 
* [敎育部異體字字典 Dictionary of Alternate Characters](https://dict.variants.moe.edu.tw/variants/rbt/query_by_standard_tiles.rbt?command=clear). 
* [小學堂 *Xiaoxue tang*](http://xiaoxue.iis.sinica.edu.tw/)
* [搜詞尋字 *Sou ci xun zi*](http://words.sinica.edu.tw/sou/sou.html). Multi-function database of Chinese characters and words, notable for its special search functions. Contains a searchable copy of the *Hanyu da zidian*. 
* [漢典 zdic.net](https://www.zdic.net/). Includes alternate character forms, various dictionary definitions, topolectal and historical pronunciations. 

Classical Chinese Dictionaries
* [Thesaurus Linguae Sericae](http://tls.uni-hd.de/home_en.lasso), a Literary Chinese thesaurus.
