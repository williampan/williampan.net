EJ Koh, "Love Appears in Three Lines", *Berkeley Poetry Review* 46 (2016), p. 54:

> A boy danced. He was the Buddha.  
At night, he shape-shifted into two rivers.  
I stole into him, then drowned. 

My favorite poem of the ones I typeset in my four years at the review.
