The night was sleepless, with helicopters overhead. I awoke before dawn, ate some toast, gathered my things, and walked to the bus stop with Henry. The air felt alive, as it often does before morning travel. 

On the plane to San Francisco, I watched *There Will Be Blood*,*Mad Max: Fury Road*, and *Dr. Strangelove*, which I later relaized was a fitting selection for a time of crisis. An oil baron leaves his black mark across America; desert colonies fight for survival in a world of endless war; vain and powerful men destroy the world with nuclear forces.
