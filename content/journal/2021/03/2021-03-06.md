In my dream I held an office job at a towering glass building that looked to be in a tropical suburb, perhaps on the fringes of Taipei. The walls were overgrown with ivy and everything was cast in a green light as if in a Tarkovsky film. People dressed in 60s work uniform walked back and forth among the cubicles. One of my coworkers from real life was there, in the midst of being fired in a very public way because of something she had said in private two years ago about wanting to eliminate her superiors and take their place. She was crying saying she no longer wanted such things, to no avail. Somehow I knew that the company had obtained this information via a little bloblike videogame creature that had followed her around and recorded her conversations, something that looked to be a Pokemon or an Animal Crossing character. Perhaps I was involved in planning this surveillance program. I had a vision of her in a small, dimly lit room under construction, the pink blob floating around her as she spoke of her bosses in rage. In any case when I exited the building she was there walking beside me, still crying, her hair blowing in the wind, the gigantic tropical trees towering around us.
