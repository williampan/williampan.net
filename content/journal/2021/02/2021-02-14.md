i was in the mountains of japan in my dream, with a group of friends i didn’t know. the trees were gray and the air was misty. we were at some sort of amusement park, constructed entirely of wood, that had carts of people and objects shuttling back and forth on wooden tracks. at the desk they had a list of activities that we could spend money on. we bought a few beers, and paid an exorbitant amount of money to play an elaborate arcade basketball game that somehow involved both basketballs and ping pong balls. they had us get in a cart and set us up in a dark booth high in the establishment. there we enacted our little ball rituals, while the inhabitants of the grounds, of which there were a few, hung laundry or walked their dogs next to us. other than that, the place was mostly empty, just the carts rolling by every so often.
