In the afternoon, Dad and I drove to the lookout point at the edge of Hope Ranch, where groups of Latino youth were gathered in their racecars and SUVs. We got out and looked at the sea then drove up Cliff and along the shoreline. The beachside parking lots were closed because of the holiday, so we drove all the way to the cemetery and walked around and looked at the tombs. Dad told some Buddhist stories as we walked. Then we drove back along the shoreline again.
