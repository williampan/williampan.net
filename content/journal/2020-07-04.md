In the afternoon, Dad and I drove to the lookout point at the edge of Hope Ranch, where groups of Latino youth were gathered in their racecars and SUVs. We got out and looked at the sea and got back in the car, then drove up Cliff and along the shoreline. The parking lot was closed because of the holiday, so we drove to the cemetery and looked at the sea and walked around. Dad told some Buddhist stories as we walked. Then we drove back along the shoreline again.
