* The work of [Tomoaki Uno](http://unotomoaki.com/) 宇野友明 (b. 1960), Nagoya
* The site of [*Taiwan Architect*](http://www.twarchitect.org.tw) magazine (建築師, founded 1980)
* The site of the [Premio Rafael Manzano](https://www.premiorafaelmanzano.com/en/), a prize for new traditional architecture in Spain and Portugal
* The work of [Juan Cuenca](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Cuenca) (b. 1934), Córdoba, whose tourist information center in Córdoba I liked when I visited
* The work of [OnEarth Architecture](http://www.oneartharch.com/index.php?s=/List/index/cid/2.html) 土上建築 (founded 2013), Beijing and Xi'an
* The work of [Emilio Tuñón](http://www.emiliotunon.com/works/) (b. 1959), Madrid
* Miscellaneous projects on ArchDaily
