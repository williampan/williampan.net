Dad drove me to the airport at daybreak for me to catch my plane to San Francisco. An older couple brought four boxes of eggs onto the plane; next to me, for the whole hour-long flight, a sorority girl beamed a photograph of herself back and forth between her phone and her laptop to make indiscernible edits to her skin. My layover in San Francisco was six hours; I spent it in a half-awake state, writing and sleeping. It is a much more pleasant airport than LAX. 

On the plane to London, I watched *The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly*, a Korean action film, and the beginning and end of *Knives Out*.
