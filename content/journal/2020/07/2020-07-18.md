Wild campus dreams---I could remember the layout clearly. There was a huge cavernous classroom building, a dorm building connected to it via a covered walkway, a basketball courts and a gigantic lawn and a shopping area beyond. It mostly took place in the cavernous building. A lecture where an ancient professor sat paralyzed at the front, a light in the back shining upon him. A man that looked like Lukas making catapult contraptions while he talked. Later, a conspiracy where a group of friends and I insulted the queen, and we had to cut sensitive parts of a poem we wrote out with scissors and hide them in our rooms. I visited one of the conspiracy friends near the end of the dream and he was in bed. The fitted sheets had snapped up and curled over and he had pulled it over himself, mumbling inside.

Afternoon: went to the pull-up bars by Puerto Vallarta to meet with Henry and used the equipment a bit and watched three muscular Hispanic men toy with their skipping ropes and crawl on the concrete and take photos for each other, then walked across the road to the beach to leap into the water. What cold water! A coldness that reaches the bones.
