Last night’s dream: what I remember began in a strange building with low ceilings. When we reached the perimeter there was a grate in the floor that glowed blue. We leapt through it onto the ground below and were suddenly in a different world, the sky a dark blue, the ground covered in long dark rushes, a river running, glowing beasts the size of dinosaurs. We stood in awe and looked at the shooting stars, then returned to the building to try to climb up it and jump through the grate again to go where we wanted to be. When we did that it was daytime, and they were preparing for a coronation. We waited indoors in a building with tall glass windows like a modernist ski lodge, then joined the procession which began in the hallways and stairways of a gray structure that had pale light streaming through the windows. There were priests from different religions, Buddhist abbots with their staffs, a queen in a Victorian dress.
