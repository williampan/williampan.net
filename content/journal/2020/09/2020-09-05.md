in last night’s dream
 i was in the audience of a symphony. they played horrifically, and everything was disorganized—a real conversation starter. made some good friends with the people next to me making fun of the stage crew galumphing around in their tuxedos. suffered laughing through it all. later we were in a cafe, talking, then we were suddenly out on the hills, throwing cherries to each other and catching them in our mouths! the air fragrant, the hills in the distance a deep fresh green after a recent fire
