Dream last night. I was in a class taught by my 8th grade geometry teacher, Mrs. Brown. The classroom was a wide open area bright with floodlights, like a school gym. I kept missing class to take cross-country trips and was devastated that I'd miss the tests and my grades would suffer. She let me make up the tests. Somehow, this involved my dad driving us to a strip mall at night to order burritos. He accidentally took the car into a narrow pedestrian tunnel, all the while admonishing us for being "bad at studies". We were all shocked by this bold statement. I remember the light streaming out from a concrete stairwell onto the blacktop. While this was happening, Jacob texted me about how he needed a copy of the test I was taking, to combine with another test that he was getting from someone else. For him, that was how academics worked.
