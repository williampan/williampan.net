Watched [*Keep Cool*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keep_Cool_(film)) (有話好好說, 1997), a black comedy set in 90s Beijing, directed by Zhang Yimou. Starring Jiang Wen 姜文 as the bookseller protagonist, Li Baotian 李保田 as the office worker, Qu Ying 瞿穎 as the girl, Liu Xinyi 劉信義 as the nightclub owner, Ge You 葛優 as the policeman, Zhao Benshan 趙本山 as the poem reciter.

The poem that Zhao Benshan recites is the lyrics to 牽掛你的人是我 (1993); the song played in the nightclub scene is Echo Bass, [Say Up and Down](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a-Em8c0qxAY). The rock songs in the soundtrack were recorded by the Beijing singer [Zang Tianshuo](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E8%87%A7%E5%A4%A9%E6%9C%94) 臧天朔 and his band, 一九8九樂隊. 

Vocab words: 掄 (lūn, to brandish), 黃花 (*adj.*, virgin, e.g. 黃花小伙、黃花姑娘), 嘎崩 (onomatopoeia), 人多眼雜, 匹夫之勇 (reckless bravado), 有勇無謀.
