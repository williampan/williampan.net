Ellwood by moonlight---gulches---cliffside woods---tales of San Francisco---a path down---beachside ruins---clear view of the oil rig---a skunk sighting---distant bonfires---seaside murder caves---up the path---unrecognizable lands---through the forest---suburban streets---back to car. 

This was a terrifying walk. Talk of ghosts and exorcism and *The Witch* and murder, as wild animals slinked about and the dark leaves rustled above us. The edge of civilization always more grotesque than true wilderness.
