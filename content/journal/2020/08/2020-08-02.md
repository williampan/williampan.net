In my dream I was a ghost of myself. I could see my real self, whom I followed around, acting independently, but we were both invisible to the guards. We were sneaking around the archives, vast golden art deco hallways, books and objects arranged haphazardly as if it were a provincial museum. We proceeded floor by floor, attempting to find a specific thing, descending via the chandeliered stairs or grillwork elevators, until we reached the luxury restaurant on the bottom floor and started poltergeisting the diners.
