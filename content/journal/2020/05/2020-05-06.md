A dream from last week---Daniel and I kept taking trains back and forth between towns. The trains were modern but very dark inside, with seats arranged in a haphazard fashion as if they were fastened incorrectly. There was a girl who I think was modeled off of Michelle who kept covering my eyes with her hands. The towns that we kept visiting were my wild dream towns---Muslim quarters with high walls of rammed earth, Italian cathedrals, contemporary malls.
