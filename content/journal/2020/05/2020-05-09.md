Took a long nap; insane dreams. I was taking someone to see the Santa Barbara Museum of Art and there was an exhibit in one of the galleries that was just piles of skulls, freshly dug up, that they claimed to be the first human skulls, discovered in Mozambique. There were headsets hung messily on the walls so we could “listen to the tapes” about the skulls. Later, there was a British person who kept pulling out modernist paintings to show me, explaining each one. We both knew they were famous paintings but I’d never seen any of them before. “Yeats” was one of the artists. After showing me the paintings he showed me a video in which he had mounted a camera to the wall, attached bungee cords next to it, and launched himself ass first with the bungee cords into an elaborate model of a city that he had set up in the far side of the room.
