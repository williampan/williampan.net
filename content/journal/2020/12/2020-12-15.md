dreams insane -- we three and payam were in an old japanese car, escaping from something deep in mountains. we started on blacktop, in a parking lot, and teens in hoodies were slinging construction items at us, things that should have been too heavy for them to wield. one of them almost got a molotov cocktail in the passenger window, which wouldn't roll up, and another nearly hooked the roof of the car with an excavator claw that he was swinging from the end of a chain.  we were in the car panicked and laughing through it all, questioning how we got in this situation in the first place. eventually we escaped that danger zone and drove on a narrow, poorly maintained mountain road until the asphalt ceased and we had to go by foot down stone steps lined with houses. people would try to block us on the way down but payam would charm his way through, sometimes speaking in farsi with them
