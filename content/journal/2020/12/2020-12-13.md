part of my dream occurred in an abandoned multi-story building with a large open plan, a sequence of big rooms chained together. there was unpainted drywall, scattered furniture, construction materials lying in piles. I was participating in a gunfight, but it was eerily quiet, as if in a deafened world -- we'd spend most of our time walking between rooms, shooting our silent guns every so often, watching the blood spray out slowly

later, I was somewhere high in the mountains, perhaps in the andes. it felt like machu picchu, but instead of ruins there was a brutalist structure unlike anything I'd ever seen, entirely new and pristine, with a giant cantilevered slab of concrete casting a crisp shadow on the mountain. a series of steps led its way up into the shallow dark area beneath the support of the cantilever, and at the outer edges at the bottom of the steps was an amphitheater and a concrete box that held a bookshop. a perfect block had been cut from the edge of the bookshop wall, just the right size to fit a specific golden book. meanwhile, a giant screen was playing a slideshow of lavishly decorated buildings that I had never seen -- glistening pyramids, caryatids, lush tropical plants
