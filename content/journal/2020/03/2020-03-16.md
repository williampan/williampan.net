I arrived at the office to discover that it's nearly empty; the company has encouraged everyone to begin working from home, starting today. 

San Francisco enters shelter in place tomorrow. Ian and his friend drove out one last time, to see the sunset from the city parks. Other friends in San Francisco posted photos too. The photographs are magnificent and unreal, as if the city is offering its brightest flame before it enters dormancy. 

Henry sent news from Oxford: someone had taken advantage of the quarantine measures to steal £10 million worth of paintings from Christ Church. The night before I left, I had heard the helicopters searching for the thieves. 

At night, Francis arrived, back from Berkeley.
