After breakfast, we took a walk in Christ Church Meadows and saw the boathouses by Oxford River: one for each college, each with its crest. University's is a dark, modernist thing at the end of the bank that they send some DPhil students to live in. I am struck by how precisely the English landscape matches that of my imagination: grey skies, vast lawns, windswept trees, water that runs with a reflective pallor that looks like it's generated from deep within. 

I napped through the afternoon. In the evening, we met with Will and Daniel to eat at Tse, a ramen shop run by a Cantonese man, then returned to Henry's flat and listened as Will and Daniel read poetry.
