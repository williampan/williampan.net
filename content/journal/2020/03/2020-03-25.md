Watched *Sun in the Last Days of the Shogunate* (幕末太陽傳), another Kawashima Yūzō 川島雄三 movie. As with his other films I am continually enchanted by the rotating cast of characters: there are samurai with deep affected voices, harlots with black teeth, dissatisfied men, a scheming grifter. A prostitute pushes a man into the sea who returns as a false ghost; the grifter rises in social status; the samurai marry a young couple on a boat and burn the Foreign Quarters. They fight and gamble, smile and deceive, moving ceaselessly within the halls of the brothel, a whole world in itself.
