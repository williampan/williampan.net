title: Taiwanese Resources
---
A list of resources for learning Taiwanese. 

Guides: 

* [General guide for English speakers](https://www.reddit.com/r/ohtaigi/wiki/index), written by A-iong
* [Taigi Phonics](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLTSKFutIq9d1YCzD8RgIjpGsR9R34-BLt), Youtube series by A-iong 

Textbooks: 

* Maryknoll Taiwanese ([Book 1](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IkSl_uZuFexvQSAY3b4lhgVsCZ935a8t/view), [Book 2](https://drive.google.com/file/d/177z9r-RD9MsHLlBV91ZXXrexTGeGc463/view), [Book 3](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LhyZMI5kBwzsl_XBVL4mv2Hb0cTDjUHV/view)). The most comprehensive textbooks for English speakers. 
* [Kaohsiung City Taiwanese textbooks](http://163.32.202.2/newholo/teach/teach_2.htm). Twelve books of six brief lessons each. Includes audio, but no vocab lists or explanations of grammar and usage. 

Dictionaries: 

* [ChhoeTaigi](https://chhoe.taigi.info/), which combines several dictionaries into one database. 
* [台字田](http://tech.fhl.net:8100), an orthographic dictionary that gives details on etymology and historical orthography.
